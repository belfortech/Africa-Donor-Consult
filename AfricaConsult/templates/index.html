Who we are

Africa Donor Consult & Procurement Auditors is a professional consulting firm specializing in procurement audits, donor compliance, monitoring & evaluation, and fraud risk assessment for donor-funded initiatives.

We work alongside international donors, NGOs, and governments to ensure every dollar spent translates into tangible results on the ground. With a team of multidisciplinary experts, we deliver high-quality, independent assessments that promote efficiency, transparency, and sustainable development outcomes.
